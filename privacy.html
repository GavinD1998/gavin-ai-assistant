Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gavin AI Assistant (the “App”), an internal-use application for KEY Home Furnishings, accesses, processes, stores, and shares information. The App is installed locally on company-controlled Mac computers and is available only to authorized personnel.

1. Scope

The App supports authorized company workflows involving invoices, receiving, Gmail, and QuickBooks Online. It is not offered to the public, does not create public user profiles, and is not a public software-as-a-service product.

2. Data the App may access

With authorization and only as needed for approved company workflows, the App may access:

  • Gmail messages and attachments related to invoices and receiving;
  • QuickBooks Online company data;
  • vendor records, the Chart of Accounts, and Terms;
  • invoice and receiving PDF documents; and
  • corrections and teaching instructions entered locally by authorized users.

The exact data available depends on the connected account, the permissions approved during authorization, and the user’s company access.

3. Local data storage

Application data is stored locally on the company-controlled Mac where the App is installed. Local records may be stored in SQLite. Managed document copies may be stored in the App’s Application Support folder, and audit logs are stored locally. Original invoice and receiving PDFs remain on the user’s Mac.

OAuth tokens and client secrets are stored in macOS Keychain rather than in ordinary application databases. The App does not intentionally place credentials in the public website or its source files.

4. How data is processed

Invoice and receiving documents are processed locally where possible. The App may use Apple technologies such as PDFKit for PDF handling and Apple Vision optical character recognition (OCR) to read document text.

Documents are not sent to a cloud artificial intelligence service unless that capability is explicitly configured in the future. If cloud AI processing is introduced, this policy and relevant company notices will be updated as appropriate before use.

The Gmail API and QuickBooks Online API are used only after an authorized user connects the relevant account and grants the requested permissions. API data is used to carry out the authorized company workflow, such as locating invoice-related messages, retrieving attachments, referencing company accounting data, or preparing reviewed accounting actions.

5. Data sharing

KEY Home Furnishings does not sell or rent data accessed by the App. Data is not used for advertising.

Data is shared only with connected services necessary for an authorized workflow, such as Google for Gmail and Intuit for QuickBooks Online, or when required by law. Those services process data under their own terms and privacy policies. The App does not create public user profiles or publish company data for public access.

6. User choices and control

Subject to company policy and access controls, authorized users or administrators can:

  • disconnect Gmail from the App;
  • disconnect QuickBooks Online from the App;
  • revoke OAuth permissions through the connected Google or Intuit account;
  • delete local records;
  • remove locally cached data and managed document copies; and
  • remove stored tokens and secrets from macOS Keychain.

Disconnecting a service prevents future API access after the authorization is revoked, but it does not automatically delete information already retained by Google, Intuit, or KEY Home Furnishings.

7. Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect information handled by the App. Connected services use OAuth authorization, and OAuth tokens and client secrets are stored in macOS Keychain instead of ordinary application databases. Access is limited to authorized users on company-controlled computers.

No method of storage, transmission, or processing can be guaranteed completely secure. Authorized users should protect their device and account access and promptly report suspected unauthorized access or data loss.

8. Data retention

Local application data is retained until deleted by an authorized user or administrator, subject to company recordkeeping needs. Historical audit information may be retained for company operations, accountability, and financial recordkeeping.

Data held by connected services may remain in Google or Intuit systems under their own retention policies, legal obligations, account settings, and backup practices. Revoking the App’s access does not control data those services independently retain.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App, connected services, company practices, or legal requirements. The effective date at the top identifies the current version.

10. Contact

Questions or requests concerning this Privacy Policy or the App’s data practices may be sent to gavin@keyhomefurnishings.com.
